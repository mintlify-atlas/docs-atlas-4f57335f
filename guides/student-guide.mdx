## Welcome to Barro Tal Vez

As a student (alumna), Barro Tal Vez helps you track your ceramic pieces through every stage of creation, view your fired works, manage your account balance, and stay updated with workshop notifications.

## First Time Login

When you first access Barro Tal Vez, you'll be required to change your temporary password for security.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Temporary Password">
    Log in with the email and password provided by your workshop administrator
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New Password">
    You'll see a welcome screen asking you to set a new password. Choose a secure password you'll remember
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Your Dashboard">
    After setting your password, you'll automatically enter your personal dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Your Dashboard

Your dashboard has four main sections accessible via tabs at the top:

- **MIS PIEZAS** (My Pieces) - Active pieces in progress
- **MIS HORNEADOS** (My Fired Pieces) - Completed pieces you've picked up
- **MIS CUENTAS** (My Account) - Financial summary and payment history
- **Profile Avatar** - Click your avatar to access profile settings

### Top Bar Features

- **Logo** - Barro tal vez branding and workshop identity
- **Bell Icon (🔔)** - Notifications (shows red dot when you have unread updates)
- **Profile Photo** - Access your profile and settings

## Managing Your Pieces

### Registering a New Piece

Whenever you start working on a new ceramic piece, register it in the system:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Mis Piezas">
    Ensure you're on the **MIS PIEZAS** tab
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Out the Form">
    In the "Nueva Pieza" (New Piece) section:
    - **Nombre de la pieza** - Give your piece a name (e.g., "Taza de Gato")
    - **Foto de la pieza** - Tap to upload a photo of your work
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Registrar Pieza** to add it to your collection
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Photo is required! You cannot register a piece without uploading an image.
</Warning>

### Understanding Piece States

Your pieces move through different stages. You can update some states yourself, while others are managed by workshop staff:

**States You Can Update:**
- **🏺 Modelado** → **💨 Secando** (Modeling → Drying)
- **💨 Secando** → **⏲️ Listo p/ Horno** (Drying → Ready for Kiln)
- **🍪 Bizcocho** → **🎨 Esmaltado** (Bisque → Glazed)

**Workshop-Managed States:**
- **⏲️ Listo p/ Horno** → **🔥 Horneando** (Ready → Firing)
- **🔥 Horneando** → **🍪 Bizcocho** (Firing → Bisque)
- **🎨 Esmaltado** → **🔥🔥 2do Horneado** (Glazed → Second Firing)
- **🔥🔥 2do Horneado** → **🎁 Lista Retirar** (Second Firing → Ready for Pickup)
- **🎁 Lista Retirar** → **🏠 Entregada** (Ready → Delivered)

### Advancing a Piece State

When you complete a stage that you can update:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Piece">
    Scroll to the piece card in your "En Proceso" list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click the Action Button">
    At the bottom of the card, you'll see a button like "Pasar a Secado" or "¡Ya Esmalté!"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The state updates immediately and you'll see a success message
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you don't see an action button, it means the piece is waiting for workshop staff to advance it to the next stage.
</Tip>

### Editing a Piece Photo

You can change the main photo only while the piece is in **Modelado** or **Creación** state:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Piece">
    Find the piece card with the photo you want to change
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Edit Photo">
    If the button **📷 Editar foto** appears in the bottom-right of the photo, click it
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select New Photo">
    Choose a new image from your device. It uploads automatically
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Adding Progress Photos

Track your piece's journey by uploading photos at different stages:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Photo Upload">
    Below the piece information, click **📸 Subir foto de avance**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Photo">
    Choose the photo file from your device
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload">
    Click **Subir 📸** to add it to the photo history
  </Step>
</Steps>

All progress photos appear in a scrollable gallery on the piece card.

### Loading More Pieces

If you have many pieces, they load in batches. Scroll to the bottom and click **Cargar más piezas** to see older pieces.

## Viewing Fired Pieces

Once your pieces are finished and picked up, they move to your gallery of completed works.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Fired Pieces">
    Click the **MIS HORNEADOS** tab
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Your Gallery">
    Scroll through your collection of finished ceramic pieces
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Details">
    Each card shows:
    - Final photo of the piece
    - Piece name
    - Pickup date
    - "Entregada" (Delivered) badge
  </Step>
</Steps>

Click any photo to view it full-screen.

## Managing Your Account

### Checking Your Balance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account Section">
    Click the **MIS CUENTAS** tab
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Main Card">
    The large card shows:
    - **Saldo a Pagar** - Your current balance (red if you owe, green if paid up)
    - **Pagos Totales** - Total amount you've paid
    - **Costos Totales** - Total charges (classes, materials, firing, etc.)
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your balance is calculated as: **Costos Totales - Pagos Totales = Saldo a Pagar**

### Viewing Charge History

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Charge History">
    In the "Tus Movimientos" section, click **Historial de Cargos**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Charges">
    A panel slides up showing all charges:
    - **Concepto** - Description (e.g., "Cuota Marzo", "Horneado pieza grande")
    - **Monto** - Amount charged
    - **Fecha** - Date of charge
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Close">
    Tap the X or swipe down to close the panel
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing Payment History

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Payment History">
    In the "Tus Movimientos" section, click **Historial de Pagos**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Payments">
    See all your recorded payments:
    - **Concepto** - Payment description
    - **Monto** - Amount paid
    - **Fecha** - Payment date
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you notice a discrepancy in your charges or payments, contact the workshop administrator.
</Tip>

## Managing Your Profile

### Viewing Profile Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Profile">
    Click your profile photo in the top-right corner
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Information">
    You'll see:
    - Profile photo
    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Phone number
    - Assigned shift (day and time)

  </Step>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    In the profile view, click **Editar Datos**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Information">
    You can change:
    - **Profile photo** - Click the camera icon on your photo
    - **Nombre Completo** - Your full name
    - **Teléfono** - Phone number
    - **Email** - Email address
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Guardar Cambios** or **Cancelar** to discard
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Logging Out

At the bottom of your profile, click **Cerrar Sesión** and confirm when prompted.

## Understanding Notifications

You receive notifications when:
- Your piece advances to a new state
- Your piece is ready for pickup
- Workshop staff makes changes to your pieces
- Important workshop announcements

### Viewing Notifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Notification Panel">
    Click the bell icon (🔔) in the top bar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Read Updates">
    A panel slides up showing all notifications with:
    - **Message** - What happened
    - **Timestamp** - When it occurred (e.g., "Hoy, 3:45 PM", "Ayer, 10:20 AM")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Clear All (Optional)">
    Click **Limpiar todas** to remove all notifications
  </Step>
</Steps>

The red dot on the bell icon disappears once you open the notification panel.

## Common Student Tasks

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I just finished modeling a piece">
    1. Take a clear photo of your work
    2. Go to **MIS PIEZAS** tab
    3. Fill out the "Nueva Pieza" form with a name and photo
    4. Click **Registrar Pieza**
    5. When the piece is dry, click **Pasar a Secado**
    6. When fully dry, click **¡Lista para Horno!**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My piece was fired and I want to glaze it">
    1. Check **MIS PIEZAS** - your piece should show as "🍪 Bizcocho"
    2. Apply your glaze at the workshop
    3. Optionally: Upload a photo of the glazed piece
    4. Click **¡Ya Esmalté!** to advance the piece
    5. Wait for workshop staff to fire it again
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I got a notification my piece is ready">
    1. Open notifications (bell icon)
    2. You should see "Tu pieza [name] está lista para retirar"
    3. Visit the workshop during pickup hours
    4. Once staff marks it as picked up, it moves to **MIS HORNEADOS**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I want to check what I owe">
    1. Go to **MIS CUENTAS** tab
    2. Look at the large number under "Saldo a Pagar"
    3. Click **Historial de Cargos** to see itemized charges
    4. Make payment to workshop administrator
    5. They'll record it and your balance will update
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I want to update my phone number">
    1. Click your profile photo in top-right
    2. Click **Editar Datos**
    3. Update the **Teléfono** field
    4. Click **Guardar Cambios**
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Understanding the Ceramic Process

Your pieces go through a specific journey from wet clay to finished ceramic:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Modeling (Modelado)">
    You create the piece on the wheel or by hand-building
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Drying (Secado)">
    The piece air-dries slowly to prevent cracking. This can take several days
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="First Firing (Horneando)">
    The dried piece goes into the kiln for bisque firing (usually 900-1000°C). This makes it hard but porous
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Glazing (Esmaltado)">
    You apply glaze to the bisque-fired piece. The glaze is powdery at this stage
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Second Firing (2do Horneado)">
    The glazed piece is fired again at a higher temperature (usually 1200-1280°C). The glaze melts and forms a glassy surface
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pickup (Entregada)">
    Your finished piece is ready! It's waterproof, food-safe (if the right glazes were used), and complete
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Each firing takes 12-24 hours including cooldown. Be patient - rushing can ruin your piece!
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't upload a photo">
    - Check that your photo file is a valid image format (JPG, PNG)
    - Ensure you have a stable internet connection
    - Try a smaller file size if the upload fails
    - The app automatically compresses large images to 500KB
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My piece state won't update">
    - Make sure you're clicking the button (not just the dropdown)
    - Check that there is an action button visible - some states can only be changed by workshop staff
    - If you see "En proceso del taller..." it means you need to wait
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I don't see a piece I registered">
    - Scroll down in **MIS PIEZAS** and click **Cargar más piezas**
    - If the piece was marked as "Entregada", check **MIS HORNEADOS**
    - Contact the administrator if it's still missing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The app says I need to change my password">
    - This appears on first login or when the administrator resets your password
    - Choose a secure password and click **Guardar y Entrar**
    - You won't see this screen again unless your password is reset
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
