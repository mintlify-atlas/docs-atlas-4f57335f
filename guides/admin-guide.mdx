## Overview

As an admin, you have full control over the Barro Tal Vez ceramic workshop. Your dashboard provides tools to manage students, track all pieces through their lifecycle, handle finances, and organize class schedules.

## Accessing the Admin Dashboard

After logging in with your admin credentials, you'll see the admin panel with three main sections accessible via the top navigation:

- **🏺 Piezas** (Pieces) - Main view for managing all pieces and finances
- **👩‍🎨 Alumnas** (Students) - Student management
- **📅 Turnos** (Shifts) - Class schedule management

## Managing Students

### Adding a New Student

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Students">
    Click the **👩‍🎨 Alumnas** tab in the top navigation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in Student Details">
    In the "Nueva Alumna" form at the top, enter:
    - **Nombre Completo** (Full Name) - Required
    - **Email** - Required
    - **Teléfono** (Phone) - Optional
    - **Asignar Turno** (Assign Shift) - Select from available shifts
    - **Foto de Perfil** (Profile Photo) - Optional
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Register">
    Click **Registrar Alumna** to create the student account
  </Step>
</Steps>

The system automatically generates a temporary password for new students, which they'll be required to change on first login.

### Editing Student Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Student">
    Scroll to the student card in the grid below, or use the "Filtrar por turno" dropdown to filter by shift
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Hover over the student card and click the **✏️** (edit) icon in the top-right corner
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Information">
    The form at the top will populate with the student's current data. Make your changes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Guardar Cambios** to save, or **Cancelar** to discard
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Payment Status

Each student card shows a payment badge:
- **✅ Cuota Al Día** (Green) - Payment up to date
- **⚠️ Cuota Pendiente** (Red) - Payment pending

Click directly on the badge to toggle the payment status.

### Deleting a Student

Hover over a student card and click the **🗑️** icon. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

<Warning>
  Deleting a student is permanent and cannot be undone.
</Warning>

## Managing Pieces

### Understanding Piece States

Every piece goes through a 10-stage lifecycle:

1. **🏺 Modelado** (Modeling) - Initial creation
2. **💨 Secando** (Drying) - Drying phase
3. **⏲️ Listo para horno** (Ready for kiln) - Dry and ready
4. **🔥 Horneando** (Firing) - First firing in progress
5. **🍪 Bizcocho** (Bisque) - After first firing
6. **🎨 Esmaltado** (Glazed) - Glaze applied
7. **🔥🔥 2do Horneado** (Second firing) - Glaze firing
8. **🎁 Lista Retirar** (Ready for pickup) - Finished
9. **🏠 Entregada** (Delivered) - Picked up by student
10. **💔 Rota** (Broken) - Damaged/broken

### Viewing All Pieces

In the **🏺 Piezas** tab, you'll see:
- Complete piece registry with photos
- Current state for each piece
- Owner (alumna) name
- Registration date
- Photo history (if available)

### Changing Piece State

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Piece">
    Scroll through the piece list or filter by student using the "Filtrar por alumna" dropdown
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select New State">
    Click the state dropdown on the piece card and select the new state
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Update">
    The state updates immediately. Students receive a notification of the change
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Editing Piece Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Edit Icon">
    Click the **✏️** icon next to the piece you want to edit
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Modify Details">
    An edit form appears at the top where you can change:
    - Piece name
    - Reassign to different student
    - Upload new photo
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save or Cancel">
    Click **Guardar Cambios** to save or **Cancelar** to cancel
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Deleting a Piece

Click the **🗑️** button on a piece card and confirm the deletion.

### Viewing Photo History

Pieces with multiple photos show a photo history gallery below the main image. Click any thumbnail to view it full-screen.

## Managing Finances

The finance management tools appear at the top of the **🏺 Piezas** view in two side-by-side panels.

### Recording a Payment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Student">
    In the "Registrar Pago" (Record Payment) panel on the left, choose the student from the dropdown
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Payment Details">
    - **Concepto** (Concept) - Description (e.g., "Cuota Marzo")
    - **Monto** - Amount in Argentine pesos
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Registrar** to record the payment
  </Step>
</Steps>

The payment is immediately reflected in the student's account balance.

### Recording a Cost/Charge

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Student">
    In the "Registrar Costo" (Record Cost) panel on the right, choose the student
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Link to Piece (Optional)">
    If the cost is for a specific piece (like firing), select it from the "Pieza asociada" dropdown
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Cost Details">
    - **Concepto** - Description (e.g., "Horneado pieza grande")
    - **Monto** - Amount to charge
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Registrar** to add the charge to the student's account
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Common costs include:
  - Monthly membership fees ("Cuota mensual")
  - Firing charges ("Horneado")
  - Materials ("Materiales")
  - Special classes or workshops
</Tip>

## Managing Shifts (Turnos)

### Creating a New Shift

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Shifts">
    Click the **📅 Turnos** tab
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Day and Time">
    In the "Nuevo Turno" form:
    - **Día** - Select day of the week from dropdown
    - **Horario** - Enter time range (e.g., "18:00 a 20:00")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Shift">
    Click **Agregar +** to create the shift
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Editing a Shift

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Hover over the shift card and click the **✏️** icon
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Modify Details">
    The form populates with current data. Make your changes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Guardar Cambios**
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Deleting a Shift

Click the **🗑️** icon on a shift card. 

<Warning>
  You cannot delete a shift that has students assigned to it. Reassign the students first.
</Warning>

## Common Admin Workflows

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Processing a kiln load">
    1. Filter pieces by state: "Listo para horno"
    2. Note which pieces are going in
    3. Change all pieces to "Horneando"
    4. After firing completes, change to "Bizcocho"
    5. Students receive notifications at each state change
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Monthly billing cycle">
    1. Go to **👩‍🎨 Alumnas** tab
    2. Review each student's payment status badge
    3. For students with pending payments, go to **🏺 Piezas**
    4. Use "Registrar Costo" to add monthly fee
    5. When student pays, use "Registrar Pago" to record it
    6. Update payment badge status on student card
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Student pickup day">
    1. Go to **🏺 Piezas** tab
    2. Filter by state: "Lista Retirar"
    3. As each student picks up their pieces, change state to "Entregada"
    4. The piece moves to the student's "Mis Horneados" (Fired Pieces) section
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Handling a broken piece">
    1. Find the piece in the **🏺 Piezas** tab
    2. Change state to "💔 Rota"
    3. The student is automatically notified
    4. Consider whether to charge for materials or firing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="New semester setup">
    1. Go to **📅 Turnos** to create new class shifts
    2. Go to **👩‍🎨 Alumnas** to add new students
    3. Assign each student to their shift
    4. Send login credentials to new students
    5. Remind them they'll need to change their password on first login
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I delete a student?">
    The student account and all associated data are permanently deleted. Their pieces will show "Desconocido" (Unknown) as the owner. Consider marking accounts inactive instead of deleting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I move pieces between students?">
    Yes. Click the edit icon (✏️) on a piece and use the "Reasignar Alumna" dropdown to transfer ownership.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do students receive notifications?">
    When you change a piece state, the system automatically creates a notification in the student's account. They see a red dot on the bell icon and can view all notifications in the notification panel.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I undo a state change?">
    Yes. Simply change the piece state back to the previous stage using the dropdown. The system doesn't restrict backward movement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I need to charge a student for something not related to a piece?">
    Use "Registrar Costo" and leave "Pieza asociada" as "-- Sin pieza --". Enter the description in the "Concepto" field.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
