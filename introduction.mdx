## What is Barro Tal Vez?

Barro Tal Vez is a comprehensive pottery workshop management system designed to streamline studio operations. It provides tools for managing students, tracking pottery pieces through their lifecycle, handling payments and billing, scheduling classes, and keeping everyone informed with real-time notifications.

Built with React and Express.js, Barro Tal Vez offers separate admin and student dashboards, making it easy for workshop owners to manage their studio while giving students visibility into their work and account status.

## Who is this for?

**Pottery Workshop Owners**: Streamline your studio operations, track student work, manage payments, and organize class schedules all in one place.

**Students**: View your pottery pieces in progress, check your account balance, review your class schedule, and receive notifications about your work.

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: React 19, Vite, TailwindCSS, React Router
- **Backend**: Express.js, Node.js
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM
- **Authentication**: JWT-based authentication with role-based access control
- **Deployment**: Docker Compose for containerized deployment
